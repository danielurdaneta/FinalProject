UFC Fight Predictor

In this project, our goal is to create a machine learning model which can predict the winner of a UFC welterweight fight using historical fighter data. We also hope to glean insight into the most successful fighter metrics such as stance and age.

Inital Analysis


Initial findings on the weight classes showed that the heavyweight class is by far the most deadly in terms of KO’s . Since their punches carry more mass behind them, they are more likely to cause serious damage to someone if they land a hit on the head. Whereas, the women-straw weight class had the least amount of KO’s due to less force and mass, showing that the punches in that weight class are a lot less likely to cause a knock out.


The Open Stance had the higher average win by TKO, indicating that a fighter that uses this stance is more likely to get a knockout. The next graph will show a different story.


Although fighters that practiced the Open Stance saw a higher average of knockouts, the Orthodox Stance and the Southpaw stance experienced the highest number of wins by KO/TKO. This can mean that masters of these two stances are more likely to be successful in their careers whereas Open Stance might be easier to pick up for newer fighters and is averaging a higher KO/TKO by stance.
